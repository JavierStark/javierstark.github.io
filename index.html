About Me

Computer Engineering student at the University of Málaga with a strong focus on scalable backend development, cloud architectures, and the emerging intersection between computing and life sciences. I specialize in .NET/C#, ASP.NET, and cloud platforms including Azure and Oracle Cloud.

My work emphasizes robust engineering practices—TDD, CI/CD, clean code, refactoring, and architectural clarity—to build production-ready systems. I've deployed full-stack applications to Azure and Oracle Cloud, always prioritizing maintainability, performance, and reliability.

I thrive in fast-paced, competitive environments, having twice won the Ada Byron Regional Programming Tournament and receiving awards at Malackathon (Excellence in Development - INDRA) and Málaga Jam for software quality, innovation, and rapid development under pressure.

Currently expanding into bioinformatics and synthetic biology as a member of iGEM UMA, complementing my research internship at Karunya University, where I built a cloud-backed EEG processing pipeline for epilepsy prediction using deep learning. As President of IAESTE Computer Science Málaga, I help students access international opportunities and cultivate a collaborative tech ecosystem.
